CPSU started as Negros Occidental Agriculture School(NONAS) and was dubbed as the 1st Agricultural Institution in the country established by a filipino Superintended name Jose P. Crisanto imeediately after World War in 1946.

The institution was convered to Negros Occidental Agricultural College(NOAC)by virtue of Presidental Authority on September 6, 1977.

NOAC then was converted into state college known as the Negros State College of Agriculture(NSCA) by virtue of R.A 9141 date July 3,2001

By virtue of the Republic Act (R.A) 10228,NSCA was converted to Central Phillipines State University in 2012
